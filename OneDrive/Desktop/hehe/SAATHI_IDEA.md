# Saathi — 10/10 Business Idea

**Tagline:** "Hire AI employee for ₹3K/month. Replaces ₹40K human. Works 24/7. Speaks Indian languages. Posts on socials. Books appointments. Collects payments."

---

## The Problem

Indian SMBs (salons, real estate, clinics, gyms, tutoring centers) lose **80% of leads** due to:
- Slow WhatsApp response (main business channel in India)
- No social media management
- Manual appointment booking
- No automated follow-up

**Pain:** ₹15-40K/month for receptionist + marketing person

---

## The Solution: Saathi

Single AI agent that runs entire customer-facing operations:

### Inbound (WhatsApp + Social DMs)
- Receives messages from WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger
- Qualifies inquiries (asks smart 3 questions)
- Books appointment in calendar
- Sends payment link
- Follows up with no-shows
- **Speaks:** Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Bengali, Kannada, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, Malayalam, English

### Outbound (Social Media)
- Posts daily on Instagram/Facebook/Google Business Profile
- Generates content from owner's voice notes
- Auto-replies to comments
- Stories with special offers
- WhatsApp broadcast to past customers

### Backend Ops
- Sends invoices via WhatsApp
- Collects payment (UPI/Razorpay)
- Daily summary: "12 inquiries, 8 booked, ₹24K collected"
- Monthly report card
